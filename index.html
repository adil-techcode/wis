01 WHY WIS

Exceptional Education and a Sense of Purpose

At MIS, we believe that when students develop a deeper sense of purpose, their academic engagement increases along with their well-being, resilience, and leadership. We encourage our students to not just study, but also to reflect on their learning, find their purpose, and celebrate in community. We achieve this through our four core values: study, reflection, community, and service.

An exceptional education—where values and academics join together—creates more than just great students. It develops great people.
